> ### Abilities
> 
> ♾️ ***Multiattack.*** **(x2)**
> 
> ♾️ ***Sneak Attack.*** **(**`3d6`**)**
> 
> ♾️ ***Strongbow.*** THe @ uses its Strength instead of its Dexterity for *Ranged Weapon Attacks.* This change is already reflected in its Weapons statistics below.
> 
> 🔷 ***Attack***. The @ *Attacks*.  
> 
> 🔵 ***Nimble Escape.*** The @ *Dashes* or *Hides*.  
> 
> 🔵 ***Rallying Cry (1/short rest, 30 ft. range).*** Up to `3` target creatures in range gains a `1d4` bonus to their *Attack Rolls*, *Ability Checks*, and *Saving Throws* for 1 minute. This bonus can only be applied once per turn.
>

> 
> ### Weapons

> ***Longsword.*** *Melee Weapon:* `+5` to hit, 5 ft. reach, Medium, Two-Handed, Finesse, `1` target. *Hit:* `1d10 + 4` Slashing or Piercing Damage.
> 
> ***Greatbow.*** *Ranged Weapon:* `+5` to hit, Range 120/320 ft., Large, Heavy, `1` target. *Hit:* `1d10 + 4` Piercing Damage.
