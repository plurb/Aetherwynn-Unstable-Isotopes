## Replicating An Ability

Some abilities may tell you to "*Replicate* the *Ability* (or its effects)". This means you use the ability as if you were the original user, regardless of whether you know or have the ability, and without spending any of the ability's costs.
* A "Spell" you cast this way is cast at the same level of the replicated spell.
* An "Exploit" you perform this way is performed at the same level of the replicated exploit.
