### Empowered Strike
*Technique (Monk)*  
**Pre-requisites:** At least 3rd level "Monk"  

🔷 **(recharge 5-6)** Make a "Martial Arts Attack" vs. *Fortitude* against target creature.

**Success:** You deal an additional `2dM [force]` *Damage*, and the target falls *Prone* in addition to the *Attack's* other effects.
