### Deflect Missiles
*Technique (Monk)*  
**Pre-requisites:** At least 5th level Monk  

⚡ **(Range 60 ft.)** — If a creature you can see targets you with one or more *Ranged Weapon Attacks*, you may spend `X` "Stamina", where `X = any number, up to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 0)`. If you do, for each *Attack*, reduce the *Damage* it deals by `XdM + your Wisdom modifier`. Then, for each *Attack* where the *Damage* it dealt was reduced to `0` this way, *Replicate* its effects, but make a "Ranged Ki Attack" vs. the specified *Defence* instead.
