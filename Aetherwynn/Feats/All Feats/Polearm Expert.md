### Polearm Expert
*Feat (Martial)*  
**Pre-requisites:** "Polearm Adept"  

Increase your *Strength* or *Constitution* by `1`, to a maximum of `20`.

◻️ **(1/turn)** — During your turn, if all *Melee Weapon Attacks* you made this turn were made using a single "Polearm", then make a *Melee Weapon Attack* using that "Weapon", however the "Damage Die" is `1d4 [bludgeoning]` instead.
