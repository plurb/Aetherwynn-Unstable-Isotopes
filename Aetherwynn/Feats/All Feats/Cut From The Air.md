### Cut From The Air
*Feat (Martial)*  
**Pre-requisites:** 12th level

Increase your *Strength* or *Dexterity* by `1`, to a maximum of `20`.

⚡ — If you are targetted by a *Ranged Attack*, you may make a *Melee Weapon Attack Roll* using a *Melee Weapon* you are wielding against the triggering *Attack Roll*.

**Critical Success:** The triggering *Attack* critically fails.  
**Success:** The triggering *Attack* fails.  
**Failure:** The triggering *Attack* is treated as normal.  
**Critical Failure:** The triggering *Attack* succeeds.
