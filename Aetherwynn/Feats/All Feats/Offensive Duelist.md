### Defensive Duelist
*Feat (Martial)*  
**Pre-requisites:** Proficiency with at least one "Sword"  

⚡ **(requires: you are wielding a "Sword")** — If another creature fails a *Melee Weapon Attack* vs. *AC* against you, you may make a *Melee Weapon Attack* using a "Sword" you are wielding against that creature. You gain a `+X` bonus to your *Attack*, where `X = your Proficiency Bonus`.

**Critical Success:** Triple the *Damage Dice* instead of doubling them.  
**Success:** Treat the *Attack* as if it critically succeeded normally.  
**Failure:** Treat the *Attack* as if it succeeded normally.  
**Critical Failure:** Trea the *Attack* as if it failed normally.
