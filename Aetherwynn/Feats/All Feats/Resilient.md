### Resilient
*Feat (General)*  
**Pre-requisites:** At least 4th level  

Increase your *Dexterity*, *Constitution*, or *Wisdom* by `1`, to a maximum of `20`.

You gain the following, based on which score you increased:
* *Dexterity* — You gain *Proficiency* in *Reflex*.
* *Consitution* — You gain *Proficiency* in *Fortitude*.
* *Wisdom* — You gain *Proficiency* in *Will*.
