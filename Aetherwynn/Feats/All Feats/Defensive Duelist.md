### Defensive Duelist
*Feat (Martial)*
**Pre-requisites:** Proficiency with at least one "Sword"  

⚡ **(requires: you are wielding a "Sword")** — If another creature targets you with a *Melee Weapon Attack* vs. *AC*, you may gain a `+X` bonus against that *Attack*, where `X = your Proficiency Bonus`.
