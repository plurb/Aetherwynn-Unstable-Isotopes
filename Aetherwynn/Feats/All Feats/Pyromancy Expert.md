### Expert Pyromancer
*Feat (Spellcasting)*  
**Pre-requisites:** At least 8th level, "Pyromancy Adept" Feat

* ♾️ — The size of *Areas of Effect* created by "Pyromancy Spells" you cast increase by up to `X` ft., where `X = 5 * the spell's level`.

* ♾️ — If an *Attack* you made using a "Pyromancy Spell" you cast critically succeeds, affected creatures and objects gain *Vulnerability* to any *Damage* you deal using that "Spell" until the "Spell" ends.
