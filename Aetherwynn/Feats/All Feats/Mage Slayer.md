### Mage Slayer
*Feat (Martial)*  
**Pre-requisites:** You are at least 4th-level.  

> *You have trained in techniques to combat spellcasters.*

♾️ — *Spell Attacks* targetting you have *Disadvantage*.

⚡ **(requires: you are wielding a "Melee Weapon")** — If a creature within reach of a "Melee Weapon" you are wielding attempts to "Cast a Spell", you may make a *Melee Weapon Attack* vs. *AC* using a "Weapon" you are wielding against them. If this *Attack* succeeds, *Counter* the "Spell", in addition to the *Attack's* other effects.
