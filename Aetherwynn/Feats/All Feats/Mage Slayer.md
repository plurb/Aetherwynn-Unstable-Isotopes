### Mage Slayer
*Feat (Martial)*  
**Pre-requisites:** You are at least 4th-level.  

> *You have trained in techniques to combat spellcasters.*

Increase your *Strength*, *Dexterity*, *Wisdom* or *Intelligence* by `1`, to a maximum of `20`.

♾️ — *Spell Attacks* targetting you have *Disadvantage*.

⚡ **(requires: you are wielding a "Melee Weapon")** — If a creature within reach of a "Melee Weapon" you are wielding attempts to "Cast a Spell", you may make a *Melee Weapon Attack* using a "Melee Weapon" you are wielding against them. If this *Attack* succeeds, *Counter* the "Spell", in addition to the *Attack's* other effects.
