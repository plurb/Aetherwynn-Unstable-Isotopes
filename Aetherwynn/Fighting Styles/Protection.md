### Protection
*Fighting Style (Barbarian, Fighter, Magus, Paladin)*  
**Pre-requisites:** —  

⚡ — If a creature you can see makes `1` or more *Attacks* against another creature within `5` ft. of you, the defending creature gains a `+X` bonus against the triggering *Attacks* until the start of your next turn, where `X = your Proficiency Bonus`.
