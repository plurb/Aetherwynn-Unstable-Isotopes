### Formation Fighting
*Fighting Style (Fighter)*  
**Pre-requisites:** 3rd-level "Fighter"

> *You're a master of fighting in tight formation. No creature can slip past your guard.*

🔷 **(requires: Concentration, you are wielding a "Polearm" or a "Greatsword")** — Until this ability ends, whenever you would make an *Attack of Opportunity* as a ⚡, you may do so as a ◻️ instead. This ability lasts until you move `5` or more ft., become *Prone*, are *Incapacitated*, or *Die*.
