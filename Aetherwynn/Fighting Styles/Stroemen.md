### Stroemen
*Fighting Style (Fighter)*  
**Pre-requisites:** At least 5th-level Fighter  

> *You can turn the momentum of a failed strike into another blow.*

◻️ **(1/turn, requires: you are wielding a "Heavy", "Two-Handed", or "Versatile" "Weapon")** — If you failed a *Melee Weapon Attack* this turn, you may make a *Melee Weapon Attack* using a "Weapon" you are wielding.
