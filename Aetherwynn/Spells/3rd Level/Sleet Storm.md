### Sleet Storm  
*3rd-level Spell (Evocation, Hydromancy)*  
**Cost:** `4` "Mana"  
**Overload:** `1` "Mana" per level  
**Components:** C, V, S, M (a pinch of dust and a few drops of water)  

> *You create an area of cold sleet and strong wind.*

🔷🔷 **(duration: 10 minutes, requires: Concentration)** — Until this "Spell" ends, a 40 ft. radius, 20 ft. tall cylinder centered on target point in range becomes *[Difficult Terrain I][DT]*, and *[Heavily Obscured][HO]*. At the end of each of your turns, make a *Spell Attack* vs. *Fortitude* against creatures this area.

**Success:** The creature falls *[Prone]*, and takes `Xd6 [cold]` *Damage*, where `X = this Spell's level - 1`.  
**Failure:** The creature does not fall *Prone*, and takes half as much *Damage*.  

---

**Classes:** Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
