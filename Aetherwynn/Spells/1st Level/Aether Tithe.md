### Aether Tithe
*1st-level Spell (Abjuration)*  
**Cost:** `1` "Mana"  
**Components:** V, S, M (an envelope)  

⚡ **(range: 60 ft.)** — If you see a creature casting a "Spell" in range, *[Counter]* target "Spell" unless its caster expends `X` "Mana", where `X = this Spell's level`.

**Classes:** Cleric
