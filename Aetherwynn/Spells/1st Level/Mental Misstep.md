### Mental Misstep
*1st-Level Spell (Abjuration)*  
**Cost:** `1` "Mana"  
**Components:** V, S  

> *You undo a single knot of aether. Just enough to stop a simple spell.*

⚡ **(range: 60 ft.)** — If you see a creature within range casting a spell in range, *Counter* target "Spell" if its of level `1` or lower. *(Cantrips are level "0")*

**Classes:** Artificer, Magus, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
