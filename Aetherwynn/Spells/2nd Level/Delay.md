### Delay
*2nd-level Spell (Abjuration)*  
**Cost:** `2` "Mana"  
**Components:** V, S  

> *You interrupt the flow of a creature's aether, delaying their spell.*

⚡ **(range: 60 ft.)** — If you see a creature cast a "Spell" within range, target "Spell's" *Casting Time* increases by `1` round if the "Spell's" level is `X` or lower, where `X = 1 + this Spell's level`.

**Classes:** Artificer, Magus, Psion, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
