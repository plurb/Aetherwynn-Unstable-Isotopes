### Zoltraak  
*2nd-level Evocation*  
**Casting Time:** 1 Action  
**Range:** 60 ft.  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous  

Make up to `X` *Ranged Spell Attacks* on up to `X` target creatures in range, where `X = this spell's level`. *On Hit:* `2d4` force damage.

**Classes:** Wizard, Sorcerer
