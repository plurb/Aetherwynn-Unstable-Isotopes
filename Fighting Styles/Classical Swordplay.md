### Classical Swordplay
*Fighting Style*  
**Usage:** Passive  
**Range:** Self  
**Requirements:** You are wielding a Sword, and not wearing *Heavy Armour* or using a *Shield*  
**Duration:** Passive  

You gain a +1 bonus to your Attack Rolls and a +1 to your AC.

**Classes.** Fighter, Magus, Monk, Ranger, Rogue
